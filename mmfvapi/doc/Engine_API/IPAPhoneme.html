Documentation

Describes phonemes by their features, essentially providing a language-independent representation of phonemes. Features follow the naming system of the International Phonetic Association (IPA) as described in the Handbook of IPA (Cambridge Univ. Press 1999) and on the IPA website at http://www2.arts.gla.ac.uk/IPA/ipa.html
