Documentation

Document units for controlling skip and pause - NOT YET SUPPORTED.

The text passed to the TTS Engine can be thought of a "document", composed of document units, organized hierarchically. The skip method can be used to skip forward or backward within and between document units. The timing of pause can also be controlled by declaring the document unit at which the speech should stop next.
