ENorm

enhancing anykind of mail text to a more understandable format.
The purpose of email normalization is to transform emails into a form that can be read out in a natural and intelligible way by the FlexVoice text-to-speech system. Only plain-text emails are handled, i.e., HTML is not.

* Important note * ENorm is just a demo application. It is based on various heuristics that work for most emails, but sometimes they make mistakes. (For example, unusually composed separator lines are not recognized, only the most frequent smileys are detected, signatures are not always recognized, etc.) Usage of the Email Normaliser

On Windows platforms, the directory \Program Files\Mindmaker\FlexVoice3 SDK\MMFVAPI\bin contains the binary.
