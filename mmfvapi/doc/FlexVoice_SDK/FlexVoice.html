FlexVoice

The main function of any TTS system is to process electronic text and provide audio output. The special properties that distinguish FlexVoice from existing products are:
  • quality (intelligibility and naturalness) of speech;
  • flexibility to provide different voices, intonation, and output formats;
  • small footprint, i.e. small distribution size;
  • low computational resources (CPU load and RAM).
Based on a unique hybrid approach, FlexVoice maximizes the advantages of the best traditional TTS implementations to offer impeccable sounding, flexible, synthesized speech that is highly intelligible yet requires remarkably low resources. Thus, FlexVoice offers an efficient and effective solution for all of today's spoken language delivery needs.

In client-based applications, FlexVoice can bring life to personal assistants, assistive tools, games, language learning programs, text readers, and translation tools. On the server side, FlexVoice is the perfect solution for call center automation, unified messaging, information services, operator services, and mobile communications services and devices.
