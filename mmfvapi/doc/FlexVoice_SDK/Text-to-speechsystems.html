Text-to-speech systems

With the increased importance of mobile computing and telephone access to Web-based services, high quality, natural sounding speech synthesis is crucial. Text-to-speech (TTS) synthesis provides a means of automatically converting written information into speech. It has the same role as a person reading aloud. TTS is particularly useful for reading text that has a high degree of variability, such as stock prices, current events, or E-mail messages. For this type of text, solutions like full pre-recorded messages or even the concatenation of pre-recorded words are impractical or unsatisfactory. TTS offers a powerful and efficent way of transmitting information via spoken language. The FlexVoice SDK is a text-to-speech SDK that allows you to create applications or products that use state-of-the-art speech synthesis.
